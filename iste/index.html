I • S • T • E

ISTE makes college growth simple:
Individuals, Skills, Team, and Explore.

Officially, ISTE is the Indian Society for Technical Education. In our college, it’s a clear path to grow as a person, build practical skills, find your best way to work, and explore the world through sessions, trips, and competitions.

What ISTE means at ADIT

A simple framework everyone can remember and act on.

I

Individuals

We see you as a unique person — your ambitions, aspirations, and dreams. You’re understood, not compared.

S

Skills

Awareness builds confidence (know your strengths and gaps). Articulation sharpens communication (know your style). Adaptability turns fear into curiosity (try new tech and new challenges).

T

Team

Discover your working style. Love teams? Great. Prefer solo like Batman? Also great. It’s not right or wrong — it’s what works for you.

E

Explore • Express • Evolve

Explore your inner self, express it in the real world, and keep evolving. That’s progress, one step at a time.

What we’ll do

Simple promises you can hold us to.

  • Know you as individuals. Start with orientation sessions that listen first.
  • Build the 3‑circle skills: Awareness, Articulation, Adaptability.
  • Help you map your working style. Team player or solo star — learn how you work best.
  • Expand your world. Learn in sessions, explore on trips, compete in groups, and grow with feedback.
