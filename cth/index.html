üèÉ Raghav

One thing I remember from Raghav is he's not a very disciplined artist, but that's the way art works.

üéµ Music

üé∂ Yashvi Christian

She's the one girl I know a bit from bridge course days but never imagined she's such an amazing flute artist.

When she started playing, she didn't give us a song name, but we all started singing because her art was so perfect that we all could recognize it.

Just like we can listen to flute music and understand what she's playing, can we understand what another person is trying to say when they're silent? Can we read between the lines? Can we understand the intent behind every action?

üé§ Krisha Patel

First, Yashvi and Krisha were playing the music of "pehle aap pehle aap" - I understand social anxiety is there, but girls & guys, go and perform! Your art forms are amazing.

She was singing a song, but the audience was getting mood swings - sometimes giving good background music by clapping, sometimes not. It looks like life where we know we have to complete the song (life) - sometimes people will appreciate us, sometimes they'll ignore us.

Nothing to take personally - when they're clapping, it's not because your art was great, it's due to dopamine hitting internally. Same way, if reactions aren't good, it's their cortisol.
